Dr. Vikram Sarabhai

Father of the Indian Space Program

The Life of Vikram Sarabhai

  • 1919: Born in Ahmadabad,Gujarat, India.
  • 1940: Attended Gujrat College, Ahmadabad Studied Natural Sciences from University of Cambridge, England
  • 1042: Published "Time Distribution of Cosmic Rays"
  • 1945: Doctorate in Cambridge University
  • 1947: Founded Physical Research Laboratory, Ahmadabad
  • 1962: President of Physics section for Indian Science Congress
  • 1963: Setup of India's First Rocket Launching Station, Thiruvananthapuram
  • 1969: Became First Chairman of Space Research Center, (INCOSPAR) now ISRO
  • 1970: President of General Conference of I.A.E.A. , Verina
  • 1971: Vice President of Fourth UN. Conference on "Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy"
  • 1971: worked on Launch of First Indian Satellite, Aryabhata
  • 1971: Established Satellite Instructional Television Experiment (SITE) Cable TV Through NASA

Innovative Institutions Founded by Vikram Sarabhai

  • PRL - Physical Research Laboratory
  • ISRO - Indian Space Research Organisation
  • IIM-A - Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad
  • VSSC - Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre, Thiruvananthapuram
  • IGCAR - Indira Gandhi Centre for Atomic Research
  • Community Science Centre, Ahmedabad
  • Darpan Academy for Performing Arts, Ahmedabad
  • Space Applications Centre, Ahmedabad (This institution came into existence after merging six institutions/centres established by Sarabhai)
  • Faster Breeder Test Reactor (FBTR), Kalpakkam
  • Variable Energy Cyclotron Project, Calcutta
  • Electronics Corporation of India Limited (ECIL), Hyderabad
  • Uranium Corporation of India Limited (UCIL), Jaduguda, Bihar
